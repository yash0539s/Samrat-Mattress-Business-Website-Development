How do I choose the right mattress firmness?

Choosing the right firmness depends on your sleeping position, body type, and personal preference. Firm mattresses are great for back sleepers, while softer ones are ideal for side sleepers.

Are Roommate mattresses suitable for all sleeping positions?

Yes, Roommate mattresses are designed to provide excellent support and comfort for back, side, and stomach sleepers alike.

Do Roommate mattresses come with a warranty?

Yes, all Roommate mattresses come with a 10-year limited warranty to ensure quality and peace of mind.

How long can I expect a Roommate mattress to last?

With proper care, you can expect a Roommate mattress to last between 8 to 10 years.
